About Me

I am a sofware engineer with a passion for coding.

Since I was little I have always had an interest in putting things together and fixing them when they were broken. Over time I discovered the world of computers and videogames. I then combined the skills I had from when I was young with my new passion. Little by little I learned how to build computers part by part, fixing them when something was broken and improving them when I found it necessary.

Constantly learning, I keep myself up to date with the latest technologies, yet always having the same spirit; building and fixing things, and solving problems. I have fun exchanging ideas and knowledge with friends and coworkers.

I look forward to bringing that energy and experience to my next project. Please call or email me to discuss how we can work together.
